Date Object.Set & Get UTC
Date对象的设置和获取UTC日期与时间










代码示例：
	<script type="text/javascript">
		var myDate=new Date();
		myDate.setUTCHours(12);
		myDate.setUTCMinutes(0);
		myDate.setUTCSeconds(0);

		var html="<p>"+myDate.toUTCString()+"</p>";
		html+="<p>"+myDate.toLocaleString()+"</p>";

		document.write(html);
	</script>
